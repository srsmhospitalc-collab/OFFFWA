Best Bikes Under ₹1 Lakh in 2026

Last Updated: June 2026 | Ex-Showroom Delhi Prices

Agar aap 2026 me daily commute ke liye bike lena chahte hain aur budget 1 lakh ke andar hai, to ye 3 bikes sabse best hain. Humne price, mileage, aur features compare kiye hain.

1. Honda SP 125 - Best Overall

Price: ₹89,500 | Mileage: 65 kmpl | Engine: 124cc

Honda SP 125 city ride ke liye perfect hai. Smooth engine, LED headlight, aur digital meter milta hai. 5-speed gearbox ke saath highway pe bhi stable rehti hai. Service cost kam hai aur resale value best hai.

Pros:

✅ Refined engine, no vibration
✅ 65+ mileage real world me
✅ Honda ka trust + 6 year warranty

Cons:

❌ Price thoda zyada Hero se
❌ Features basic hain

2. Hero Splendor Plus XTEC - Best Mileage

Price: ₹79,900 | Mileage: 70 kmpl | Engine: 97.2cc

India ki sabse zyada bikne wali bike. Splendor ka naam hi kaafi hai. XTEC variant me digital meter, Bluetooth, aur USB charging milta hai. Maintenance cost ₹500/month se kam.

3. Bajaj Platina 110 - Best Comfort

Price: ₹74,200 | Mileage: 70 kmpl | Engine: 115cc

Lambi seat aur soft suspension. Gaon ki sadkon ke liye best. 115cc engine hone ke baad bhi 70 mileage deti hai. 5-speed gearbox milta hai is price me.
